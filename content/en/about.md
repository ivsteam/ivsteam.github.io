## Company Overview

**Invesume** is a compound word of "Investment + Prosumer" and has grown as a public software (open source) solution specialist since its founding in 2015. Based on more than 12 years of technology and expertise, we provide various products and services including Sovereign AI Platform, Open OS, Remote Management Solutions, and WebRTC Solutions.

### Vision and Mission

**Mission:** Opening Code to Empower the AI Era

**Vision:** Leading digital innovation through open source software and AI technology to help Korea secure digital sovereignty and leap forward as a global technology leader

### Core Values

1. **Openness:** Actively utilize and contribute to the open source ecosystem
2. **Innovation:** Leading cutting-edge technology through continuous R&D investment
3. **Trust:** Providing trust with proven technology for over 12 years and public institution supply records
4. **Partnership:** Becoming a true partner that grows together with customers

## Key Achievements

- **12+ Years** Open Source Leadership
- **250K+** HamoniKR OS Downloads
- **GS Grade 1** Software Quality Certification
- **100+** Government/Public Institution/Enterprise Partners
